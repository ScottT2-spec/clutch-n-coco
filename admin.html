📖 How to Use This Admin Panel

▼

Adding a Product:

  1. Tap "+ Add Product" below
  2. Fill in the product name, category, price, and availability
  3. Add a description (optional but recommended)
  4. Add an image — tap "📁 Upload Image" to pick from your gallery, or "📸 Take Photo" to use your camera
  5. Tap "Save Product" — it'll appear on the website instantly

Editing a Product:

  1. Find the product in the list below
  2. Tap "Edit" — the form will open with the current details
  3. Change whatever you need and tap "Save Product"

Deleting a Product:

  1. Find the product and tap "Delete"
  2. Confirm — this cannot be undone

Tips:

  • Products show on the website immediately after saving
  • Use "Available" for items you have in stock, "Preorder" for items that need to be ordered
  • Include the currency in the price field (e.g. GH₵ 85)
  • Keep descriptions short — 1-2 sentences max
  • Tap "← View Site" at the top to see how your shop looks to customers
